Products

The McAuslan brand identity and legacy has been certainly high in popularity throughout Montreal because of their loyalty to keep new flavors reminiscent of the style of other ones by taking feedback from customers and company staff. Their brand recognition status is only getting better so they are capitalizing on the situation with merchandise of several different categories. They have accessories (soaps, bags, bottle openers, bottles, bells, keychains), apparel (shirts, tank tops, vests, hoodies), caps, tuques, and glasses to sell along with their unique taste. Having slick, eye catching logos and designs is essential to competing with other brands for better awareness.
